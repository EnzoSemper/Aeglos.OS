Aeglos OS: The AI-Native Microkernel

Our flagship platform is a ground-up operating system designed for defense applications that demand real-time AI inference without cloud dependency. Every layer — from hardware abstraction to application runtime — is engineered for security, speed, and reliability.

Aeglos OS integrates a full LLM inference stack directly into the kernel, enabling warfighters to access AI decision support in disconnected, intermittent, and limited (DIL) communications environments.
